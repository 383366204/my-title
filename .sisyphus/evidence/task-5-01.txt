- generateTitles behavior:

  • Calls glmClient.generateTitles({ coreWord, modifiers, peerTitles, products, maxLength }).

  • Deduplicates and returns up to 5 titles (3-5 target).
  • If GLM fails, falls back to a simple degradation: coreWord + concatenated rigid modifiers, no spaces.
